About Us

Bookcase Database is your go-to web application for book organization and exploration. With our user-friendly interface, you can effortlessly craft personalized virtual bookcases, tailored to your unique interests and reading habits. Whether you're an avid reader, a collector of diverse interests, or simply seeking to streamline your reading list, Bookcase Database empowers you to curate specialized book collections and import new titles seamlessly through the Google Books API.

Dive deeper into your reading experience by customizing book details, assigning ratings, and jotting down personal notes, transforming your bookcases into dynamic digital companions. Embark on a literary adventure where every book has its place and every interest finds its expression.

As it currently stands, bc.db does not bring anything new to the table, but stay tuned for exciting new features in the near future.

  • Image Recognition Automatic Uploads: Upload photos of your own bookshelf and watch as the books are automatically added to your bookcase.
  • Personal Recommendations: Tired of spending too much time deciding what to read and not nearly enough time actually reading? Bookcase Database will do the legwork for you and keep a constantly updated recommendation list.
  • Chat with Your Favorite Characters: Ever wonder what it would be like to have a chat with your favorite characters from your favorite books or the authors who wrote them? Bookcase Database will make that dream a reality!
  • Data Exporting and Seamless Integration with Other Applications: Bookcase Database will allow you to export your data to other applications, such as Excel, and will also allow you to import data from other applications into your bookcase.
