What is Bookcase Database?

Bookcase Database is your go-to platform for effortless book organization, where you can create customized virtual bookcases for your various interests and discover new titles through the Google Books API. Elevate your reading experience by personalizing book details and notes, making each bookcase a dynamic companion on your literary journey.

How do I use Bookcase Database?

Getting started is a breeze! Create your first bookcase below, then simply start searching for books to add. Have a rare book that's not in Google's database? No problem! There's an option to add books manually inside each of your bookcases. Then click on each book to view and edit its details.
